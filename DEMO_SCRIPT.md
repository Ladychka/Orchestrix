# Demo Script — 3 Minute Pitch

## Setup (before going on stage)
1. Ensure the deployed dashboard is open on the presentation laptop.
2. Ensure your phone has Telegram open with the bot conversation.
3. Have your email inbox visible (or a second tab) to show the sent quote.

---

## The Pitch

**1. Intro (15s)**
> "This is our AI Finance Officer — an AI employee that handles incoming quote requests end-to-end, with full human oversight."

- Show the dashboard home page. Point out the configured AI employee.

**2. Trigger a task (10s)**
> "A customer emails a quote request. Let's simulate that."

- Click the **"Trigger Demo Task"** button on the dashboard.
- Narrate: "The task is now received and the agent is working."

**3. Watch the agent think (30s)**
> "Watch the trace live — this is the agent's reasoning trail."

- Click into the new task.
- Narrate each step as it appears:
  - "It's checking inventory…"
  - "Calculating pricing with a bulk discount for 200 units…"
  - "Drafting the quotation email…"
  - "Now it stops — it knows it cannot send email without human approval."

**4. Human approval via Telegram (30s)**
> "The approver — me, or any manager — gets a Telegram message."

- Show your phone: the Telegram message arrives with quote summary and **Approve / Reject** buttons.
> "I can review the summary, and with one tap, approve it."

- Tap **Approve** on your phone.

**5. Completion (30s)**
> "The email is sent automatically, and the dashboard updates in real time."

- Switch back to the dashboard: status changes to **completed**.
- Show the email inbox: the quotation email arrived.

**6. Close with trust (15s)**
> "Every decision the agent made is logged — nothing happens without a human sign-off. This is how we build trust in autonomous AI employees."

- Scroll the step trace one more time for emphasis.

---

## Backup Plan
If Telegram or email fails during the demo:
- Show the step trace as the primary "wow" moment.
- Explain: "In a live environment, this would trigger Telegram and email; today the trace proves the agent logic is correct."
